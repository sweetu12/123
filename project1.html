3D Printed Custom Chess Piece

Project Overview

This project involves designing and 3D printing a custom chess piece using SolidWorks and FDM 3D printing technology. The goal was to create an aesthetically pleasing and functional piece, optimizing design for strength and material usage.

Design and Manufacturing Process

The design process started with conceptual sketches, followed by detailed modeling in SolidWorks. Challenges included ensuring structural integrity while minimizing material usage. The final design was 3D printed using an FDM printer, achieving the desired balance between aesthetics and functionality.
